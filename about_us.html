Our Mission

Our Mission is to bring our delicious treats to everyone. We strive to bring our cheap prices to low-income communities and give everyone the chance to purchase them. While doing this we are also able to support the local communities by giving half of all revenue to local food banks.

Locations

Cookies & S'more is rapidly expanding with new locations being added every week! Currently our biggest locations are New York , Philadelphia , and Washington D.C.
